Munoz Family Vacation - Spring Break 2018

Beavers Bend State Park (Near Broken Bow, OK)

This year's Spring break took us to Broken Bow lake in Beavers Bend State Park. About 10 miles north of Broken Bow, Oklahoma, this destination was close enough for us to drive and take the dogs with us.

We spent Sunday to Friday at "Owl Always Love You" cabin, offered by Blue Beaver Luxury Cabins, enjoying the great outdoors, with the benefits of a luxury cabin in the woods.
